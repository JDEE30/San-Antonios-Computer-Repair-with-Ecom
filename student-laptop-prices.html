How expensive are laptops
for students and professionals

For developers, content creators, and students the price of a new computer can vary depending on where you get your device, what you need your device to do and how fast you need your device to perform particular tasks. A good rule of thumb is a student will spend about $100 - $300 on a new laptop or desktop, whereas a professional will likely spend double that amount or more in most cases.

Best content creation laptop

A professional computer can mean several different things. Do you need a computer for programming? Are you coding full-stack applications?

Will you be watching media, editing media, perhaps making video content for any of the big media outlets such as Youtube or Tick Tock?

If the answer is yes and you are likely going to be editing video content, you will need something with a higher-end GPU, plenty of rams, and of course a decent processor.

Best laptop for developers

If you are solely looking for a laptop to code your projects with you could use a raspberry pi, but let's be realistic you want something nice to take with you if you get tired of the desk or office right? Well, there are a few things to consider.

If you will be doing front-end development, or game development where animation is a must, or using software like unity, blender, or other game engines you will need something with a decent GPU, RAM, and of course a good processor. You also want to make sure you have plenty of hard drive storage to make sure you don't lose your saved projects.

One of the issues we ran into initially, was never having enough storage for all the different websites, games, and apps we develop. So we ended up saving everything on Github, which is nice. But if there is ever any issue. It is always nice to have copies available.

This also allows you to have plenty of templates, graphics, vectors, logos, editing software, and other tools you may need for your projects. Imagine needing to migrate a 40GB website and you have no space left on your laptop. It's just so inconvenient.

That being said. For my projects, I find that these laptops are both affordable and more than adequate to get the job done.
